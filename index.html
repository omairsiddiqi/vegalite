Index of Community Socio-Educational Advantage (ICSEA)

  • A value on the index represents the average level of educational advantage enjoyed by the school's student population in comparison to other schools.
  • The ICSEA offers a numerical scale that measures the degree of this influence, or level of educational advantage, and takes into account factors at both the student and school level.
  • ICSEA is essentially an index that measures the average level of educational advantage received by the student population of the school in comparison to other schools.
